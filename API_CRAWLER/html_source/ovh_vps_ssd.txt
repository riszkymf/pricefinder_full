<h2 class="white center" qtlid="1130574">OVH-style high availability</h2>
<p class="white" qtlid="1130587">The security of your data is vital to us. This is why we use Ceph, a data storage and redundancy technology.</p>
<p class="white" qtlid="1025723">Thanks to a semi-random algorithm used for the distribution of storage blocks inside one or several clusters of physical hard drives, data is never stored within a single critical point thus ensuring the sustainability of the storage solution even if several storage units may require some hardware maintenance. Access to your data is thereby guaranteed and so is its integrity.</p>
</div></div>
<div class="row"><div class="col-l-8 col-s-12 col-s-center marginBottom80 marginTop40">
<h2 class="white center" qtlid="1025736">Pay-per-use storage</h2>
<p class="white" qtlid="1025749">While your projects are increasing in size, it is not uncommon for the data stored on your servers to start feeling a little tight. Until now, the only alternative was to upgrade to a superior model only to get more disk space, which is rather unprofitable in actuality.</p>
<p class="white" qtlid="1130600">To deal with these limitations, additional disks for VPS are offered as a custom solution, available in 4 versions ranging from 50 to 500 GB depending on your needs.</p>
<p class="white" qtlid="1025775">Highly secure thanks to a triple data redundancy within our infrastructures, additional disks can be activated on demand and will allow for progressive scalability.</p>
